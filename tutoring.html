Tutoring

As a teacher, I love helping people to build up their understanding. Working one-to-one is often the best way to figure out what works best for a particular individual. Which sort of explanation will lead to an "Aha!" moment for them? Are there some hidden misconceptions that need to be uncovered and overcome? Time-permitting, I have some availability for one-to-one tutoring:
  • Math (middle school, Algebra 1, Geometry, Algebra 2, Precalculus, Calculus, Linear algebra)
  • Coding (Python, Matlab, javascript)
  • Data science (statistics, computational data analysis, libraries such as NumPy and scikit-learn)
  • Test-prep (math and English sections of SHSAT / ISEE / SAT / ACT / GRE)
This can be online, or in-person within NYC. If you are interested, please send me an email: rajeev dot raizada at gmail dot com .
